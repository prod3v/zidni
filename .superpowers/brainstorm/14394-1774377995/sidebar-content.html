Sidebar Widgets

The magazine layout has a sidebar next to the post list. What should go in it?

1

Categories

List of blog categories (برمجة، مشاريع تخرج، دراسة...) with post counts

2

Recent Posts

Latest 4-5 posts with small thumbnails — keeps visitors exploring

3

Newsletter Signup

Email subscription form — "اشترك في نشرتنا البريدية". Do you have a mailing list service?

4

Popular Tags

Tag cloud showing trending topics (Python, Java, مشروع تخرج, etc.)

5

Services CTA

Promote your services — graduation projects, assignments, academic translation. Drives business.

6

Social Links

Follow us on Twitter, Instagram, etc. with follower counts or icons

This is multi-select — click all the widgets you want in the sidebar. I'd recommend 3-4 max to avoid clutter.
