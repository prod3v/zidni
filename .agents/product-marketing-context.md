# Product Marketing Context — Zidni (زدني)

## Product

- **Name**: Zidni (زدني)
- **URL**: TBD (migrating to Cloudflare Workers Pages)
- **Type**: Arabic-language educational blog for university students
- **Tech Stack**: Astro 6.0+, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, MDX
- **Hosting**: Cloudflare Workers Pages (planned migration)
- **Language**: Arabic (RTL), Modern Standard Arabic
- **Font**: IBM Plex Sans Arabic

## Business Model

Content-led services business. The blog is the top-of-funnel — drives organic traffic via SEO, builds trust and authority, then converts visitors into paying clients for academic services.

```
Blog Traffic (SEO) → Trust & Authority → Service Awareness → Paid Clients
```

## Services Offered

1. **Graduation Projects (مشاريع تخرج)** — Building and delivering graduation projects for students
2. **Assignments & Homework (واجبات جامعية)** — Completing university assignments
3. **Academic Translation (ترجمة أكاديمية)** — Translating academic papers, abstracts, and research
4. **Other academic services** — Expanding over time

## Target Audience (ICP)

- **Primary**: Arabic-speaking university students
- **Demographics**: 18-25 years old, undergraduate and graduate students
- **Geography**: Arab world (MENA region)
- **Fields of study**: Computer Science, IT, Engineering (primary); expanding to other faculties
- **Pain points**:
  - Struggling with technical courses (Java, databases, algorithms, etc.)
  - Time pressure from multiple assignments and exams
  - Need help with graduation project ideas and execution
  - Difficulty translating academic work between Arabic and English
  - Lack of quality Arabic-language academic resources online
- **Language**: Arabic speakers who search in Arabic for academic help

## Content Strategy

### Content Pillars (5)

1. **دليل المواد الجامعية (Course Guides)** — 33% of output (20/month)
   - Course summaries, explanations, tutorials in Arabic
   - Covers: Java, Python, databases, data structures, algorithms, OS, networks, web dev
   - CTA: Assignment help services

2. **مهارات الدراسة والنجاح (Study Skills)** — 20% of output (12/month)
   - Study techniques, time management, research writing, exam prep
   - Broad appeal, shareable content
   - CTA: "Focus on what matters, let us handle the rest"

3. **مشاريع التخرج (Graduation Projects)** — 20% of output (12/month)
   - Project ideas by language/field, planning guides, presentation tips
   - Highest commercial intent — directly sells graduation project service
   - CTA: Project delivery service

4. **الترجمة الأكاديمية (Academic Translation)** — 13% of output (8/month)
   - Translation guides, common mistakes, tools, terminology glossaries
   - Directly sells translation service
   - CTA: Professional translation service

5. **الحياة الجامعية والمسار المهني (Student Life & Career)** — 13% of output (8/month)
   - Career prep, CV building, freelancing, in-demand majors, certifications
   - Community building, shareable
   - CTA: Follow Zidni / soft brand awareness

### Publishing Volume

- **Target**: 60 articles/month
- **Writer**: Sole author (Mohammed) — building phase

### Conversion Strategy

- In-content CTA boxes after "hard part" sections
- Persistent services sidebar/banner
- End-of-article CTAs
- Dedicated service landing pages:
  - `/خدمات/مشاريع-تخرج`
  - `/خدمات/واجبات-جامعية`
  - `/خدمات/ترجمة-أكاديمية`
